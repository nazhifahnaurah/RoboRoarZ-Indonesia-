RoboRoarZ Indonesia 2025: Battle of Smart Racers

An exciting robotics competition that challenges the speed, intelligence, and strategic thinking of robots on an autonomous racing track.

In this competition, participants will use robots provided by the organizers, which must be programmed and controlled to race with high precision and reliable navigation capabilities.

Battle of Smart Racers is not just about speed — it’s a proving ground for logic development, sensor algorithm programming, and efficient problem-solving strategies. Robots must be able to follow lines, navigate tracks, and overcome various technical obstacles prepared along the course.

This competition encourages participants to sharpen their programming skills, think critically, and solve problems creatively, while also fostering a spirit of collaboration and healthy competition.

Don’t miss this incredible opportunity — register your team now and be part of the race!
