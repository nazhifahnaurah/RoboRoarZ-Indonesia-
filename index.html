About

RoboRoarZ is a design-centered reconfigurable robotics competition that fosters creativity, innovation, and collaboration among the participants. All the participants will compete using the SUTD-designed reconfigurable robotic platform "Smorphi".

Our goal is to establish a vibrant local and international community that unleashes the power of reconfigurable robots in solving real-world challenges.

  • The competition is open to the participants from senior high school.
  • Each team must have 3 to 5 members or be 15-19 years old.
  • A Smorphi robot will be provided at no cost for all competing teams.
